About WildFellas

WildFellas is a wildlife tour and photography experience brand dedicated to bringing people closer to the raw beauty of nature through immersive and meaningful journeys.

Built on a shared passion for wildlife and storytelling, WildFellas brings together Amar Atitkar, Jitesh Deshpande, and Kalindi Koparde — combining on-ground expertise with creative direction, design, and marketing to craft well-rounded experiences.

We curate journeys across India's iconic national parks, including Kanha, Tadoba, Pench, Jim Corbett, and Panna, with a strong focus on responsible tourism and ethical wildlife practices.

WildFellas is not just about travel — it's about discovery, learning, and building a deeper connection with the natural world, while fostering awareness, respect, and conservation of wildlife.

The People Behind It

Meet the Fellas

03 Co-Founders
01

Amar Atitkar

Co-Founder · Wildlife Photographer
"The forest became my language — where silence speaks, and every moment tells a story waiting to be captured."

Wildlife photographer and nature enthusiast from Pune. Journey began in 2008 at Kanha National Park — a single safari ignited a lifelong passion.

Being hearing-impaired shaped a unique connection with the wild — the forests became a language of observation beyond sound.

Explored India's iconic wildernesses — from Kanha and Tadoba to Pench, Jim Corbett, and Panna — documenting their beauty through the lens.

02

Jitesh Deshpande

Co-Founder · Expedition Lead
"From lines of code to trails through the forest — the best algorithms are written by nature itself."

Backbone of the company and its biggest cheerleader. Leads business strategy while contributing creative thinking to everyday operations.

Former IT professional who left coding for VFX, channelling that creative energy into WildFellas — balancing structure, vision, and creativity.

03

Kalindi Koparde

Co-Founder · Creative & Marketing
"Design is how I speak — and the forest is where I find my deepest creative voice."

Pune-based design professional with over 9 years of experience in graphic design and art direction. Leads marketing and branding at WildFellas.

WildFellas is more than travel — it's a way of reconnecting with creative roots by becoming one with the forest.
