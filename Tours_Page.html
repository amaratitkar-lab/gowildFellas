Reptiles & Amphibians

Herping

Monsoon & post-monsoon: Herping peaks during and just after the monsoon when frogs, snakes, and lizards are at maximum activity. Night walks included on all trips.

01
Matheran
Raigad · Maharashtra
India's only car-free hill station. Night walks through misty forest reveal geckos, Malabar pit vipers, and endemic frogs.
Upcoming →
02
Amboli
Sahyadri · Maharashtra
India's herping capital. Multiple endemics — Amboli Toad, Amboli Bush Frog, and Malabar Gliding Frog.
Upcoming →
03
Phansad
Coastal Sanctuary · Maharashtra
A hidden coastal sanctuary. Indian monitor lizard and star tortoises in coastal woodland — rarely visited, deeply rewarding.
