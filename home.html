From the Wild

"
I'd never been on a jungle safari before, mainly because I'd heard there's no guarantee of spotting wildlife. But joining a tiger safari in Pench National Park with WildFellas turned out to be unforgettable.

We spotted deer, monkeys, birds, wild boars, jackals, and even two leopards before an incredible moment unfolded — a tiger appeared barely 10–12 feet away from our jeep and calmly walked behind us for nearly 10 minutes. Later, we learned she was a pregnant tigress.

That experience completely changed my perspective. It was thrilling, humbling, and brought me much closer to nature. A truly memorable safari with WildFellas.

Dr. Rohan Kulkarni

Wildlife Safari Guest · Pench 2025
